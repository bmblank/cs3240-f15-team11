Mission Statement

SecureShare: An Information System to Support Secure Information Sharing
Many small groups in the world need a means to collect, share, and distribute information securely, without risking it falling into the wrong hands on their own computer or servers, and without it being lost due to other reasons (theft, viruses, etc).

SecureShare.V1 provides a platform for reporters, consumers, and world audiences alike to securely publish and trade information, publically or privately, over our highly encrypted servers, delivering state of the art peer-to-peer (P2P) communication
